Annual Purchasing Review

The procurement division of Northern Logistics approved several acquisitions during Q2.

A purchase order associated with reference INV-99881-X was finalized after review by management.

Among the approved items were:

- 23 Lenovo ThinkPad laptops purchased at $1,150 each
- 45 wireless keyboards purchased at $35 each
- 10 docking stations purchased at $220 each

The total authorized spending amounted to $30,225.

The acquisition was made on April 17, 2025 on behalf of Northern Logistics Group.

All expenses were approved and recorded in the company ledger.
